Crowns and veneers start at $4 USD per unit. Full Arch from $25 USD. Surgical guides from $59 USD per implant. Aligners from $25 USD per set. No minimum order and no contracts. We quote per case before starting any work.

Is the STL compatible with my milling machine?

Yes. We export standard STL compatible with all major CAM software: XTCERA, Cerec, Roland, Datron, Wieland, VHF and most open-system mills. We can also deliver Exocad .constructionfile if you use exocad CAM.

Standard cases are delivered within 24–48 hours. Simple cases with clear prescription can be ready from 15 minutes. Surgical guides require 24h for CBCT viability review plus 24–48h for planning and design.

Do you work with labs in USA, UK and Australia?

Yes. The service is 100% digital. You send the scanner file, we send back the STL design. We work with labs and clinics across USA, UK, Australia, Canada, Spain, and anywhere in the world. Payment in USD via international transfer or PayPal.

Do you design aligners and surgical guides too?

Yes. Aligner treatment planning (full STL series per stage) from $25 USD per set. Surgical guides for implants using CoDiagnostiX, Exoplan, RealGuide or BlueSkyPlan from $59 USD, including CBCT viability assessment and full digital planning.
